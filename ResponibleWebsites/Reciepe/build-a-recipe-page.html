Tea Reciepe

Tea or chai plays a vital role in indian's daily life

This Reciepe is the so called perfect tea makijng reciepe

Ingredients

  • Tea leaves
  • Water
  • Kettle
  • Sugar

Instructions

`
  1. Boil Water - Heat water in a kettle or pot until it reaches a boil.
  2. Add Tea - Place a tea bag or loose tea leaves in a cup or teapot.
  3. Pour and Steep - Pour the hot water over the tea and let it steep for 3 -5 minutes.
  4. Serve - Remove the tea bag/leaves, add sugar or milk if desired, and enjoy!
